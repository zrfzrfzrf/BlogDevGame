## Reflection
This week, we held a meeting to discuss the specific design details of our game. We adopted Elinor's proposal and decided to create a 2D, top-down, pixel-style game. There will be two players controlling the virtual characters Mi and Mo. During the exploration process, they will solve puzzles and defeat monsters to complete the game. The members of our team actively cooperated and provided their own suggestions for some key gameplay and details.

We conducted a Concept presentation. Our next goal is to create a demo.
