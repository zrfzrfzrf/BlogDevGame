## Reflection
We held two meetings this week.

The first time was a broad-based brainstorming session. We contemplated all the possible types of games we could design, including city-building games, couch cooperative games, tower defense type games, 3D open-world games, and so on. We discussed what tasks could be assigned, such as 3D modeling, game logic, website design, music, etc. Finally, we made a game list and asked the team members to try out these games at home as much as possible, or watch the video versions of them, and evaluate their development and presentation difficulties.

The second meeting was held two days later. During this meeting, we decided on the specific type of game to be developed - a couch cooperative game. Taking Hollow Knight as an example, we decided to develop a metroidvania game. We designed the background story of the game; envisioned some key features of the game; discussed the necessary platforms and tools for developing the game; finally, we still compiled a game list, which includes some classic Metroidvania games. We will continue to try these games to obtain development inspiration.
