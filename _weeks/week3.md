## Reflection
This week our team began the actual design work. Our roles were clearly defined: some were in charge of the game logic, some were responsible for the graphic design, some for the music design, and I was the overall technical director, responsible for the creation, management of the code repository, and most of the programming work.

We conducted a face-to-face joint work session for this week's demo presentation. I underestimated the complexity of implementing our idea - when working on the linked objects, we encountered significant problems with time management (because synchronizing objects for operations such as passing through walls required complex kinematic and dynamic conversions across two parallel spaces). As a result, we were unable to present a complete first level during the demo.
